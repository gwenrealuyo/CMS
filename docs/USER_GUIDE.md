# The Lighthouse — User Guide

**Audience:** Church staff, coordinators, teachers, pastors, and members who use the app day to day  
**Last updated:** June 2026

**PDF:** `User-Guide.pdf`

---

## Welcome

**The Lighthouse** (LAMP Church Care System) helps your church shepherd people from first visit through discipleship. You can manage people and families, track small groups (clusters), run evangelism and formation programs, schedule events, and more — all in one place.

What you see in the app depends on your **role** and any **module assignments** your administrator has given you. If a menu item is missing, you may not have access to that area.

---

## Getting started

### Sign in

1. Open the church CMS in your browser.
2. Go to the **Login** page.
3. Enter your **username** and **password**.
4. Click **Sign in**.

If this is your first login with a temporary password, you will be asked to **change your password** before continuing.

### Forgot your password?

1. Click **Forgot password** on the login page.
2. Follow the instructions to contact an administrator.
3. An admin can reset your password from your profile or from **Admin Settings → Password Resets**.

### The main screen

After login you land on the **Dashboard**. Around the screen you will find:

| Area | What it does |
|------|----------------|
| **Left sidebar** | Jump to People, Clusters, Evangelism, Lessons, and other modules |
| **Top search bar** | Find people, families, clusters, events, and more |
| **+ menu** | Quick actions (Add Visitor, Submit Report, Create Event, etc.) |
| **Bell icon** | Notifications (overdue reports, follow-ups, activity) |
| **Profile menu** | My record, Account settings, Sign out |

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard shows key numbers for your church — active members, visitors, baptisms, cluster attendance, NCC progress, upcoming events, and recent activity.

- **Cluster coordinators** often see a **Submit Report** shortcut here.
- Cards and numbers respect your **branch** and **access level** — you only see data you are allowed to view.

## People and families

### Directory (People)

Use **People** in the sidebar to browse the church directory.

- **Search and filter** by name, branch, role, or status.
- **Open a profile** to see contact details, role, branch, family links, and the **Journey** timeline (baptism, cluster attendance, lessons, events, notes, and more).
- **Table / Cards** toggle changes how the list is displayed.

**Who can add or edit people?**

| Your access | What you can do |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Admin or Pastor** | Add and edit any person in your scope |
| **Module coordinator** (any module) | Add and edit people in your scope |
| **Member** (no coordinator assignment) | Add **Visitors** only; view self and family |

Use **+ → Add Person** or the **Add Person** button on the People page.

When creating someone who will log in, an admin sets a **temporary password**. Share username and password securely.

### Families

Use **People → Families** to manage households.

- Create a family, assign a **family leader**, and add members.
- Link families to **clusters**.
- View members who are not yet in a family from the unassigned list.

## Clusters (small groups)

Clusters are your church's small groups. Open **Clusters** in the sidebar.

### Tabs

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **Clusters** | View and manage cluster details (members, families, coordinator, schedule) |
| **Reports** | Browse submitted weekly reports |
| **Compliance** | Oversight view for senior cluster coordinators |

### Submit a weekly cluster report

Cluster coordinators submit a report after each meeting:

1. Go to **Clusters** or use **Dashboard → Submit Report** / **+ → Submit Cluster Report**.
2. Select your **cluster** (if you manage more than one).
3. Enter **meeting date**, **gathering type** (Physical / Online / Hybrid), and **attendance** (members and visitors).
4. Add activities, prayer requests, testimonies, offerings, highlights, and lowlights as needed.
5. **Save** the report.

Attendance you record is reflected on each person's **Journey** timeline.

### Members and clusters

Regular **members** can view their own cluster in **read-only** mode. They cannot edit cluster settings or submit reports unless they have a cluster coordinator assignment.

## Evangelism

Open **Evangelism** for Bible study groups, visitor follow-up, and conversion tracking.

### Tabs

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **Groups** | Bible study / evangelism groups (leaders, members, cluster link) |
| **Each 1 Reach 1** | Annual conversion goals by cluster |
| **Tally** | Monthly people tally (Invited, Attended, NCC, Baptized, Received HG, Reached) |
| **Reports** | Weekly evangelism reports |
| **Bible Sharers** | Which clusters have evangelism facilitators |

### Common tasks

- **Create or update a group** — coordinators add leaders, members, and meeting details.
- **Track prospects** — invited visitors until they attend; mark **Attended** when they come.
- **Record conversions** — water baptism and Holy Ghost reception dates update the person's profile and Journey.
- **Submit a weekly report** — evangelism coordinators use **Reports** or **+ → Submit Evangelism Report**.

---

## Lessons (New Converts Course — NCC)

Open **Lessons** for the seven-lesson New Converts Course and student progress.

### Tabs

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **Lesson Content** | Course lessons (read-only for most users) |
| **Student Progress** | Who is assigned which lesson and completion status |
| **Session Reports** | One-on-one teaching sessions |
| **Commitment Forms** | Signed commitment PDFs per student |

### Typical workflow for teachers / coordinators

1. **Assign lessons** — Student Progress → select students → **Assign Lessons**.
2. **Log a session** — Session Reports → record date, lesson, student, and notes.
3. **Track commitment** — upload or mark commitment form signatures on the Commitment Forms tab.

Progress moves through: **Assigned → In Progress → Completed**.

---

## Sunday School

Open **Sunday School** to manage classes by age category.

- **Categories** — Kids, Teens, Young Adults, etc.
- **Classes** — teachers, room, meeting time, yearly theme.
- **Enrollment** — add students individually or in bulk.
- **Sessions** — schedule classes (including recurring sessions).
- **Attendance** — tied to calendar events; present / absent / excused.

Teachers see their classes; coordinators see broader class lists and summary stats.

---

## Events

Open **Events** for the church calendar.

- Switch between **calendar** and **list** views.
- Filter by type (e.g. Sunday Service, Doctrinal Class), month, or year.
- **Create an event** — coordinators use **+ → Create Event** or the Events page button.
- **Take attendance** — open an event occurrence and use the attendance panel.

Recurring events (e.g. weekly Sunday Service) generate occurrences you can open individually.

---

## Ministries

Open **Ministries** to see teams serving in worship, outreach, care, logistics, and more.

- Browse by category and meeting cadence.
- View primary and support coordinators and team members.
- Coordinators can create and update ministry records.

---

## Finance

**Finance** is available to **Admins** and **Pastors** (and finance module coordinators where enabled).

| Area | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Donations** | Record gifts with receipt numbers, purpose, and payment method |
| **Offerings** | Weekly service offerings |
| **Pledges** | Track pledges and contributions toward goals |

Use date filters on the overview to review giving for a period.

---

## Analytics

**Analytics** (Admins and Pastors) provides charts and reports on engagement, demographics, baptism milestones, and more. Some sections are still being expanded; **Engagement** and **People** tabs have live data.

## My record and account settings

### My record (`/me`)

Non-visitor members can open **My record** from the profile menu to see their own full profile and Journey timeline.

### Account settings (`/profile`)

Update your name, email, photo, and password.

**Visitors** do not log into the main app — they exist as records for staff to track.

## Notifications

The **bell icon** shows alerts such as:

- Overdue cluster or evangelism reports
- Password reset requests (admins)
- Follow-up reminders
- Recent activity confirmations

Visitors do not receive in-app notifications.

## Tips and troubleshooting

| Situation | What to try |
|-----------|-------------|
| Cannot see a sidebar module | Your admin may have disabled it, or you lack access — see the Access Levels Guide |
| Cannot edit a person | You may be a plain Member; coordinators and pastors have broader edit access |
| Cannot submit a cluster report | You need a **Cluster** module assignment for that cluster |
| Branch filter is locked | Some roles can only view their own branch's data |
| Stats cards hidden | Summary stats are limited to coordinators, pastors, and admins in some modules |
| Module missing entirely | Ask an admin to check **Admin Settings → Module Controls** |

## Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Branch** | A church location or campus |
| **Cluster** | A small group / cell group |
| **Journey** | Timeline of spiritual milestones and activity on a person's profile |
| **NCC** | New Converts Course (seven lessons) |
| **Module coordinator** | Extra access assigned per module (e.g. Cluster Coordinator, Lessons Teacher) — not a login role by itself |
| **Prospect** | A visitor invited to evangelism who has not yet attended |
| **Senior coordinator** | Module-wide oversight role with broader access |
| **V2B** | Visitor to Brethren — conversion pipeline analytics |

## Need admin help?

Account creation, password resets, and module assignments are handled by **Administrators**. See the **User Management Guide** for admin procedures, or the **Access Levels Guide** to understand what each assignment allows.

For technical issues (site down, login errors), contact your church's system administrator or developer.
